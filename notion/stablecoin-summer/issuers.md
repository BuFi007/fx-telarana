# Stablecoin Issuer Program

Stablecoin Summer gives currency issuers a way to bootstrap real usage for their stablecoin markets.

## What Issuers Get

- Dedicated market launch.
- Co-marketing campaign.
- Borrow/supply analytics.
- Campaign dashboard.
- Wallet and frontend integration.
- Issuer-specific rewards week.
- Cross-chain distribution through supported spokes.

## Example Campaigns

- JPYC Week.
- MXNB Week.
- AUDF Week.
- ZCHF Week.
- KRW1 Week.

## Issuer-Funded Incentives

Issuers may fund rewards to support:

- Borrower rebates.
- Supplier rewards.
- Cross-chain activation.
- Referral campaigns.
- Market-maker programs.

## Goal

Turn stablecoins into active credit markets, not idle balances.
