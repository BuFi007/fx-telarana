# Stablecoin Summer Rewards

Stablecoin Summer does not require a native protocol token.

Rewards may include:

- USDC cashback.
- Borrow-rate rebates.
- Fee rebates.
- Issuer-funded incentives.
- Referral rewards.
- FX Miles.
- Partner rewards.
- Early access to new markets.

## Rewarded Behavior

Users may earn rewards for:

- Supplying USDC.
- Depositing USDC as collateral.
- Borrowing supported FX stablecoins.
- Maintaining healthy positions.
- Entering from supported spoke chains.
- Completing cross-chain intents.
- Referring real users.
- Participating in issuer-specific market weeks.

## Not Rewarded

We do not reward:

- Raw bridge volume.
- Instant deposit/withdraw loops.
- Max leverage.
- Wash deposits.
- Fake borrow loops.
- Unsafe wrapped assets.

## FX Miles

FX Miles are campaign reputation points.

They may unlock:

- Fee discounts.
- Higher limits.
- Partner rewards.
- Early access to new markets.
- Future campaign tiers.

FX Miles are not a token and do not represent equity, debt, or a claim on protocol revenue.
