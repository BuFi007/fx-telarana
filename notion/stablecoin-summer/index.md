# Stablecoin Summer

Stablecoin Summer is a campaign to turn stablecoins into programmable FX credit.

Deposit USDC from supported chains, enter the FX Hub, and access global currency markets through stablecoin lending, borrowing, and cross-chain intents.

## Core Thesis

Stablecoins are no longer just digital dollars.

They are becoming the collateral layer for global FX credit.

## What Users Can Do First

- Enter from supported spoke chains with USDC.
- Deposit USDC as collateral.
- Supply USDC as lending liquidity.
- Borrow supported FX stablecoins from hub markets.
- Earn rewards for real protocol usage.

## Current Source Asset

USDC only at the spoke layer.

Other FX assets are hub-market targets first. Native cross-chain support for AUDF, JPYC, MXNB, KRW1, and ZCHF will come later through dedicated deployments and canonical routes.

## Supported Spoke Chains

- Ethereum Sepolia.
- Arbitrum Sepolia.
- OP Sepolia.
- Polygon Amoy.
- Unichain Sepolia.
- World Chain Sepolia.
- Arc Testnet.
- Fuji self-loop.

## Campaign Slogan

Earn in dollars. Borrow the world.
